Here's a time line of Netaji's life:

  • 1897: Subhas chandra bose is born to a hindu family in Cuttack, India.
  • 1911: Bose passes his matriculation exam to get into presidency college. Bose only studied there briegly, however, as he was thrown out for assaulting one of his professors for the latter making an anti-india comment.
  • 1918: passed for his B.A. in Scottish Church College under the unversity of Calcutta.
  • 1919: Bose takes the Indian Civil Service exam due to a promise with his father. he passes the exame but turns down teh job bcause he must work under the British.
  • 1919: Bose leaves India for Great Britain.
  • 1923: Elected leader of the All India Youth Council and secretary of the Bengal State Congress.
  • 1930: Bose becomes mayor of calcutta.
  • 1938: Bose accepts nomination as the INC presidential candidate.
  • 1939: Bose organizes the All India Forward Bloc.
  • 1941: Bose escapes from India after it becomesto hostile towards him.
  • 1945: Bose is announced to have died in a plane crash on the way to Taiwan, but this has been hotly disputed and never confirmed.
